## Ingredients


Ingredients for 2 persons as side dish or 1 person as main dish.

*   1 chicoree
*   cottage cheese
*   2 sun dried tomatoes in oil, chopped
*   nature yoghurt
*   olive oil
*   lemon juice
*   salt and pepper

<!-- ![Fennel Salad](fennel-salad.jpg) -->

## Preparation

Put a few spoons of yoghurt in a bowl, add olive oil and lemon juice, salt and pepper. Blend together gently.

Cut the chicoree in small rings, 1cm thick. Chop the dried tomatoes.

Add the chicoree, dried tomatoes, and a few spoons of cottage cheese to the bowl and mix with the sauce.

Enjoy.
